10 Best Celebrity Beauty Tips, According to Vogue's YouTube Channel

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, April 14, 2024

Having perfect skin demands a routine of skin care, drinking lots of water, and also eating healthy. Of course, everyone has a trick that helps to have glowing skin or just to have the best makeup results. Celebrities are in use to be in front of the cameras, and it is no surprise that they also have some tricks that are suitable for everybody.

RELATED:  10 Celebrities With Their Own Makeup And Skincare Lines

Vogue has a series of videos with celebrities giving excellent tips for skincare. Unsurprisingly there are fancy products, but almost all of them also feature things everyone can do at home.

10 Rihanna

It all started with a Rihanna's video in 2018. It was the first time this type of videos produced by Vogue went viral, and now it became a fixed content. The singer gave tips on how she uses contour to make her forehead and double chin look smaller. "I learned how to contour when I gain weight. Believe it or not, that is when it comes in,' she says in the video. Rihanna also explains she makes a triangle with the contour under the skin to make her neck look longer.

Although it went viral, and many people in the comments could relate to Rihanna, as few Black celebrities have made videos so far.

9 Cindy Crawford

Cindy Crawford is a timeless beauty icon, and she shared some secrets of her beauty routine on Vogue. Every morning, the model puts two golden balls in cold water and then uses them under her eyes, and she moisturizer the skin after that. By the way, it seems to be the main secret of everyone on this list.

The supermodel said she uses foundation, but she is not a fan of powder. "I am not a big fan of powder as I get older because it tends to settle on those fine lines of wrinkles."

8 Alicia Keys

Alicia Keys loves her beauty routine, and it seems a relaxing moment for her. The singer revealed that she likes to be quiet in her bathroom, light some candles while taking care of her skin. She always starts with a cleanser and applies an aloe vera gel mixed with powder. She also puts lots of attention in the region under the eyes, and she uses a fancy Lancer Legacy Eye Treatment Duo. Of course, she never forgets about sunscreen!

RELATED: Here's What Emilia Clarke Looks Like With No Makeup

Keys also said that for her, the most important thing in her routine was to start drinking lots of water, and it did make a difference in her skin. She also said that skincare was frustrating for her because, for a long time, she didn't have good skin, and it was a long process until, she found the right products.

7 Liv Tyler

Liv Tyler is another celebrity who is obsessed with skincare. She says that it was something that her mother and grandmother loved to do, and she learned a lot from them. The actress has sensitive skin, so she uses an oily cleanser. "To me, the secret for beautiful skin is exfoliation," she says.

The Lord Of The Rings star also loves facial masks, and one of her favorites is called "Blue Flame." After that, she uses a moisturizing mask, and after cleaning the face, she adds a few drops of hyaluronic acid. She also revealed that everything she uses on her face, she also put on her hands. The actress also uses a toll to make a massage on her face. When she gets puff eyes, the star uses eye pads, that she keeps in the fridge.

6 Alessandra Ambrosio

Alessandra Ambrosio likes to start her day with a face mask, and she uses a jade roller to settle it on her face. The model revealed that she keeps the roller on her fridge and helps her wake up. After that, she applies a face mist that she cares about in her bag. After that, she adds sunblock, glow drops, and tint a moisturizer.

The model adds a light foundation, and she uses her fingers to apply it, and a concealer. Ambrosio loves to pay attention in the details and uses an eyebrow gel and lip tint.

5 Joan Severance

Joan Severance is a supermodel from the '80s, and she is aging beautifully. The model shared some secrets for her skin, and she always starts with cleaning and scrubbing the skin. Severance also uses a face mask, a cleanser, toner, and serum.

The model also gave useful tips for makeup. It is great to see Vogue putting senior people under the spotlight.

4 Priyanka Chopra

Priyanka Chopra is the definition of perfection. The actress shared some secrets she learned from her grandmother. She scrubs her lips with salt, vegetable glycerin and rose water, and who wouldn't love to have lips like that?

She mixes gram flour, yogurt, sandalwood powder, turmeric, and a few drops of lemon for the body. Sometimes, she adds milk until she gets the right consistency. The actress says she applies it on her body and waits until it is dry before washing it off.

3 Camila Mendes

Camila Mendes always takes some minutes to take care of her skin before going to bed. After cleaning her face, she uses a face mask for a few minutes, and then she ads a detox mask.

After washing her face, the actress adds a toner and a moisturizing serum. After that, she uses a massage roller that she keeps in the fridge. It seems like a secret most of the stars on this list love!

2 Kendall Jenner

Of course, the Kardashian/Jenner clan also has some videos sharing their secrets. For example, Kendall Jenner explained how she does her makeup, and it is something that everyone can do daily. She just puts foundation, concealers, and brushes her eyebrows. "I don't like to have too much one on because I hate to have kinky makeup," she explained.

Jenner also uses some blush and a bronzer as eyeshadow. It looks like the perfect makeup for a normal day.

1 Bella Hadid

Bella Hadid gave some tips for people who suffer from tired skin due to jet-lag. Luckily it also works to anyone who got a tired look, even if it is not for a busy schedule routine. For the model, foundation and concealer are the key-tolls to help her to get a fresh look. She adds some contour on her cheeks and under the chin, and the skin is done.

After that, it is just using some highlighter and do the rest of the makeup.
