Ximena Morales Cuellar Bio, Job, Height, Ex, Kids, Age

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 30, 2024

It’s nothing new for the ladies in 90 Day Fiance to be scrutinized; especially when the question came about them wanting to marry into money. Likewise, Ximena Morales Cuellar who appeared on 90 Day Fiance: Before the 90 Days season 5, coupled with Mike Berk, couldn’t escape the fan’s eyes.

Thus, here we are with this Ximena Morales Cuellar Bio, exploring her personal life and clearing all your queries. 

90 Day Fiance: Ximena Morales Cuellar And Mike Berk’s Relationship

Ximena Morales Cuellar was surfing through an international dating app in early 2020 when she first stumbled upon Mike Berk. Since Ximena only spoke Spanish at the time, and Mike wasn’t fluent in it, the two then bonded with the help of a translator app.

Also, it was around that time, Mike had applied for the casting of 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days. Later, as he received a confirmation call, his friends told him that he was “crazy”, and everyone reminded him about how “people are always looking out for money and things like that.”

But Mike insisted that Ximena was “definitely not like that,” and proceeded with his plan to go on and meet her. The last saw him, Mike was planning to propose and bring Ximena and her sons to the U.S.

So, what was the factor that reeled in Mike towards Ximena? He had a one-word answer for it, her “personality.”

As of 2021, the pair were still together. But given the language barrier and other obstacles like closed borders and travel restrictions, it’s a hard fight for them. Also, there’s was the question, if Ximena was more in love with the gifts and money, or with Mike himself.

The only way to find out if the two ever make it is to keep watching 90 Day Fiance: Before the 90 Days that premiered on Dec. 12, 2021.

Ximena Morales Cuellar Job

Whatever Ximena Morales Cuellar’s job might have been before, it’s in the past. She is now a reality and can easily pass for a model if she desired.

Besides that, Ximena was also reportedly involved in the clothing business before 2021.
