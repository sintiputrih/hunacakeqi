Rob Lowe Wife, Age, Net Worth, Sons, Movies

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, April 10, 2024

Robert Hepler Lowe simply called Rob Lowe is an American actor, producer and director. He is also a podcaster. Just at 15 years old, Rob Lowe made his acting debut with ABC’s short-lived sitcom, A New Kind of Family.

Rob Lowe wife

Is Rob Lowe married? Who is Rob Lowe’s wife? Rob Lowe is married to a woman named Sheryl Berkoff. She is a makeup artist. The two have been married since 1991.

Rob Lowe age

How old is Rob Lowe? Rob Lowe is 57 years old; born on March 17, 1964.

Rob Lowe net worth

How much is Rob Lowe worth? How rich is Rob Lowe? Rob Lowe has quite a lot of million dollars to his name. He has an estimated net worth of around $100 million.

Rob Lowe sons

Does Rob Lowe have children? How many sons does Rob Lowe have? Rob Lowe has two sons. They are John Owen Lowe and Matthew Edward Lowe.

Rob Lowe movies

What are some of the popular movies by Rob Lowe? Rob Lowe has starred in quite a lot of movies. Some of them include;

  •  The Outsiders (1983)
  • 9-1-1: Lone Star (since 2020)
  • Youngblood (1986)
  • Wayne’s World (1992)
