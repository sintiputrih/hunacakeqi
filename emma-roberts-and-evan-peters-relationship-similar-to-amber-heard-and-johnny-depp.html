All the talk at the moment is about Amber Heard and Johnny Depp's relationship. Whether it be Depp hugging his attorney or James Franco entering the mix, fans want to discuss everything related to the trial. RELATED - Are Amber Heard's Parents Team Johnny Depp?Emma Roberts life completely changed after she broke things off with Garrett Hedlund, however, prior, she was in another relationship alongside co-star Evan Peters. Let's take a look at that relationship and what went on between the two.

Emma Roberts And Evan Peters Have A Bumpy History

Roberts and Peters started to date back in 2012 when they met on the set of 'Adult World'. Of course, working alongside one another would continue in 'American Horror Story'. Eventually, the relationship would start to turn south, with Roberts getting arrested in 2013 for giving Peters a bloody nose.

It was said according to TMZ that Roberts also looked as though she had scratches on her legs, nonetheless the situation was swept away by Roberts and Peters' reps, issuing this statement.

“It was an unfortunate incident and misunderstanding,” Roberts and Peters’ reps told Us Weekly at the time. “Ms. Roberts was released after questioning and the couple are working together to move past it.”

In addition, Peters would refuse to press charges following the encounter.

From then on, it was a very on and off style of relationship. The two got engaged only to call it off and then get back together. Recently, Roberts faced another heartbreak, breaking it off with Robert Hedlund. It is believed that having a child during the pandemic created extra stress for the two.

Going back to Roberts and Peters, it is interesting to note that both sides have stayed very quiet about their time together.

Emma Roberts Has Kept Quiet About Her Past With Evan Peters

"I never want to talk about relationships I'm in or that are ending or have ended. It's hard enough to be with someone by yourself, let alone with an audience," she said.

Yes, Emma Roberts tends to be very private when it comes to her relationships. According to the actress, social media has not made things easy for actors in her type of situation.

"Growing up is hard. Sometimes it makes me sad that I can't have a private moment. Because of Twitter and Instagram, there's a whole other element where everybody can comment on what you're doing and no one knows the real story," she continued. "I think I know what's true, and I know what happened in my own life and so do the people who love me and who I love."

Roberts might be hinting at the fact that not all stories from the media relating to her breakup are accurate, but she seems to be okay with that, given she knows her truth.

Roberts would further say that life is filled with ups and downs, but it is all about managing those emotions.

"I think that no matter who you are or what you do or wherever in the world you are, anything ending is hard. Losing something is hard."

"I'm realizing that life is highs and lows. I'm trying to ride them out and live somewhere in the middle. When you're low, you think it's never going to end. When you're high, you're so scared of it ending. And I've lived in both of those places for too long."

Ultimately, it seems as though her relationship was much different in comparison to Amber and Johnny's.

The Breakup Was Said To Be Civil Than Johnny Depp And Amber Heard

Some fans might be quick to label these relationships in the same category however, that would be completely wrong. Verbal abuse was especially harsh in the Depp and Heard relationship, along with several physical altercations between the two. Aside from one instance, there really wasn't a history of physicality or verbal abuse between Emma and Evan, it just seems like two stars who were younger at the time and filled with emotions.

In addition, it is said that when the two finally broke up for good, they did so on amicable terms... clearly, that was not the case for Johnny and Amber, nor will it ever be.
