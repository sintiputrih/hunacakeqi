Career

Cardi B started her career as an actress in the American television industry. In the year 2015, she started with the cast of the VH1 reality TV series ‘Love and Hip Hop New York’ starring in 6 seasons. On 30 December 2016, she announced that now she wants to further pursue a career in music.

In November 2015, her first musical debut was released which was a single titled ‘Boom Boom’ on the Jamaican Reggae fusing singer Shaggy’s remix with the Jamaican dancehall singer Popcaan.On 15 December 2015, she made her first music video with ‘Cheap Ass Weave’.

In 2016, she endorsed with ‘Romantic depot’, a lingerie store from New York that markets on wellness and sexual health products. All this was presented in an article by the New York Post titled ‘Cardi B’s meteoric rise from stripper to superstar’ in April 2018.

Her second mixed tape was released on 20 January 2017 called ‘Gangsta Bitch Music, Vol.2’. Later in February 2017, she also endorsed Gypsy Sports and MAC Cosmetics for an event for New York Fashion Week.

She is the first female rapper to have the first 3 entries in the Top 10 of the Hot 100 list. This was after she collaborated with ‘MotoSport’ and ‘No Limit’. On 3 January 2018, she starred in ‘Bruno Mars’ which is the remix version of ‘Finesse’. This one reached Top 3 on the Hot 100 list in New York and Canada.

On 6 April 2018, her debut studio album was released titled ‘Invasion Of Privacy’, which acclaimed universal acclaim from music critics. Editors from ‘Variety’ called it ”One Of The Most Popular Debuts Of The Millennium”, and this was also appraised by ‘The New York Times’. This one was a masterpiece and broke a lot of records.
