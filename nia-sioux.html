Nia Sioux - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Single, Nationality, Career, Facts

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Table of Biography

Nia Sioux is an American dancer who gained reality fame as a regular on ‘Dance Moms’. Nia Sioux released her first single “Star In Your Own Life” in 2015.

Early Life and Education

Nia Sioux is 21 years old. Her full name is Nia Sioux Frazier. She was born on June 20, 2001, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA.

She is the daughter of Evan Frazier Sr.  (father) and Holly Hatcher Frazier (mother). Moreover, she has two siblings, Evan Jr., and William.

She earned a scholarship for being the most improved student in 2012. Recently, she graduated high school on June 8, 2020.

Career and Professional Life

Nia began dancing at the young age of 3. She mostly danced contemporary and musical theater numbers during her time at the Abby Lee Dance Company.

However, she made her Dance Moms debut at the age of 10. She is also a gifted singer and has been in several viral videos.

In 2016, she made her Off-Broadway debut in a New York City production of Trip of Love. She continues her dance, singing, and modeling.

Moreover, she stars as Emma Barber on the TV series ‘The Bold and the Beautiful’ and as ‘Sonitas’ in the film The Code.

She created her app called “Nia’s Nation”. Moreover, in 2016, she was cast as a dancer in “Trip of Love”.

She has appeared on the cover of several magazines including; Big City Kids and Sesi Magazine.

Further, she has also been in several Todrick Hall music videos. She was in Freaks Like Me, Straight Otta Oz, and Ease on Down the Boulevard.

Likewise, she has released CD singles for her songs “Winner”, “Star In Your Own Life”, “Slay”, “Here For You”, and “Dance (Just Rock)”.

She also made an appearance in Alexx Calise’s song “Breathe”. In June 2016, she performed in a concept video with co-star Wilson Mayo.

The following video, “Young Love,” was a dance film which was directed by Ryan Parma and choreographed by Richard Elszy.

She is also a famous social media star. Her self-titled YouTube channel was created on April 25, 2015. Her Instagram account has crossed over 5.5 million followers.

Similarly, she joined her Twitter account in August 2012. Moreover, she has her website.

She is also a popular TikTok star with more than 5.2 million followers. She received her Golden YouTube PlayButton on May 22, 2020.

About ‘Dance Moms’

On Dance Moms, she was often placed at the bottom of the pyramid.

Her mother, Holly, often got into arguments with Abby for not giving her enough attention and believed that the choreography she was given wasn’t at a “competitive standard”, as well as accusing Abby of deliberately typecasting her in different ethnic roles.

She and her mom were original members of the ALDC junior elite competition team when it was created.

Both of them remained permanent members up until the Season 7 episode ‘Ashlee’s Big Decision Part 1’ when they officially left the Abby Lee Dance Company with the other elite dancers and formed ‘The Irreplaceables’.

As the longest-tenured dancer to ever feature on Dance Moms, she was best known on the show for her signature death drops, her stage presence, her determination to win, displaying vast improvements in her technique year after year, portraying an endless variety of different characters on stage, and her strengths in doing partner works.

Personal Life

Concerning her personal life, Nia was dating a social media star, Bryce Xavier. He is also a dancer and a performer.

The couple met in the year 2017 through dance itself and became friends. They began their relationship on June 28, 2017.

Moreover, she revealed her love in an interview with ‘Teen Vogue’. However, they end their relationship in mid-2018. As of now, she is single and living her life to the fullest.

Likewise, this talented personality has never been into any drastic rumors or controversies. She has maintained a good profile and has always been dedicated to her career.
