Michael Holmes Jr. is a Canadian professional contractor. Besides, he is a TV host, public speaker, healthy home advocate and an educator, following in his father’s footsteps.

Michael Holmes Jr. Married Life & Baby

The HGTV member is a married man and his wife’s name is Lisa Maria Holmes. Michael and his wife delivered their first child named Azura Vie Holmes on 26th June, 2023. The reality star took his social media to officially announce the news of pregnancy, on 14 January 2023. Michael wrote, “Our next big adventure begins June, 2023”. 


As per HGTV, the adorable two got married on 9 February 2019. Only close friends and family members were part of an intimate function that took place in Ontario’s Blue Mountain region. Before getting married, he proposed to Lisa on a surprise Ottawa road trip where they spent time hiking the Gatineau Hills. They walked along the Rideau Canal and went for a couple massages and ate gluten-free poutine.

Insights On Michael Holmes Jr. & Wife’s Wedding

Michael’s wife-to-be wore a wedding dress designed by a close friend before wearing it down the aisle. On the other hand, Michael’s dad gifted him new cufflinks for his big day. While he was all dressed in a dapper Hugo Boss tuxedo, he had a teary eyed during the ceremony, when he first caught sight of his beautiful bride. 

Likewise, the bridesmaid dresses were ivory, rose gold and blush that defines elegance, pretty and feminine for a garden-themed wedding. His wife-to-be’s grandmother, Teri Scallon officiated the picture-perfect ceremony who flew all the way from British Columbia per wiki.

Both Michael and Lisa chose a secret garden theme to get married because they admire spending time in the great outdoors. Lisa is a herbalist and prefers finding beauty and peace in nature. All they wanted was their wedding to be in a peaceful and relaxed setting. With torches, flower petals lining the aisle and candles that dot the string of twinkle lights and property hanging in the reception tent, the wedding venue looked enchanting. 

The wedding venue has now become a special place for both families and Lisa’s family had visited and loved the area for years, before choosing the wedding venue to be in the Blue Mountain region.

Michael Holmes Jr. HGTV Popularity & More

Michael has been one of a prominent crew member of HGTV’s popular shows like Holmes in New Orleans, Holmes Inspection and Holmes Makes it Right. During his appearance in Mike’s Ultimate Garage, he was given the ultimate challenge to build his state of the art garage. Besides shining as a leader matching his skills with his father Mike Sr. the junior possesses great wit and humor. 

He is part of DIY Network series, Holmes: Next Generation that features his sinister, Sherry Holmes and their dad. They together appear in the popular HGTV hit show Home to Win. During the CTV new series Holmes Family Effect that aired in 2021, the family had a mission helping young people to make a positive change and difference into their lives and community. 

Some of the other interests and passions about Michael are carpentry and woodworking. He displays his skills through various different projects for homeowners. Apart from that, he empowers and motivates youth in the skilled trades through his work with Skills Ontario, Skills Canada, Conestoga College, and Habitat for Humanity’s GTA Young Professionals Build.

Michael Holmes Jr. Bio & Family: Wiki, Age, Height
