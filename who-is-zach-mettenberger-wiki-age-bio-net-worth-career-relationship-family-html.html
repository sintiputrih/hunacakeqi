Zach Mettenberger’s Football Odyssey

  • Early Days: Zachary Rich Mettenberger was born on July 16, 1991, in Watkinsville, Georgia.

  • Draft Selection: The Titans chose him in the sixth round of the 2014 NFL Draft after his standout performance in college football for LSU.
  • Titans Stint: Mettenberger played for the Titans for two seasons, where he showcased his football prowess as a quarterback.
  • Final NFL Season: His NFL journey concluded as he spent his final season as a backup for the Pittsburgh Steelers.
  • Memphis Express: In 2019, he joined the Memphis Express of the Alliance of American Football (AAF) for his final season of professional football.
  • Coaching Path: Following his playing days, he embarked on a coaching career, starting as an offensive coordinator and eventually transitioning into a coaching analyst role.

Family Life and Personal Milestones

Football Career Highlights
