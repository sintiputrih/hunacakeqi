Peter Mark Kendall top movies, TV shows and awards

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 15, 2024

American actor Peter Mark Kendall has worked on stage, in movies, and on television. He is most known for his performances as Richard Onsted in CBS’s Strange Angel and Hans in FX’s The Americans.

Kendall studied jazz guitar at McDaniel College and graduated with a BA in theatre. In 2013, he earned an MFA in acting from Brown University.

Peter Mark Kendall top movies and TV shows

After landing the character of Tom Henney in the television series The Leftovers, Kendall’s acting career officially got underway. Following that, he was cast in Chicago Med, The Americans, and Girls.

He portrayed the part of Connor in Time Out of Mind, which marked his film debut. In the Broadway productions of The Harvest, Blue Ridge, Mercury Fur, and The Rose Tattoo, Peter co-starred with Allison Janney in the play Six Degrees of Separation. With Hickory Collective, Kendall has also written music for movies, podcasts, and commercials.

Peter Mark Kendall awards and nominations

Kendall has no nominations yet, he is however the ambassador of many brands.
