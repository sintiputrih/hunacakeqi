Aleisha Allen Age, Biography, Career, Height, Movies and TV Shows

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Aleisha Allen is widely known as an American actress, model, social media personality, and speech-language pathologist. She is widely known for appearing in the movie Are We There Yet? Some of the movies and TV shows she has appeared in include You’re Nobody ’til Somebody Kills You and School of Rock.

Aleisha Allen started her career when she was 4 when she appeared in free print ads, however, she got her breakthrough when she featured in the TV series Blue’s Clues in 1996, a Nickelodeon show, and later got the chance to appear in other TV show, Out of The Box.

Aleisha Allen biography

Aleisha Allen was born on April 28th, 1991 in Bronx, New York, United States of America and as of 2022 she is 31 years old and currently lives in New York City, New York, United States of America.

She holds American nationality and her ethnicity is Black. Aleisha Allen believes in Christianity and her zodiac sign is Taurus.

The actress’ parents are Angela and Otis LaNae Allen. She has a younger brother identified as Omarri. With Aleisha Allen’s education, she graduated from Professional Performing Arts High School in Manhattan in 2009 where she majored in Musical Theatre but following her graduation, she changed her mind and decided to pursue acting.

Following her high school education graduation, she enrolled in Pace University, a private university based in New York, and graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Communication Science and Disorders in 2013. She later enrolled in Teachers College, Columbia University where she majored in Speech-Language Pathology and graduated with Communication Science and Disorders.

Aleisha Allen also acquired other certifications like the Fiberoptic Endoscopic Evaluation of Swallow, Speech Language Disabilities, and Certificate of Clinical Competence.

Aleisha Allen measurements

Aleisha Allen stands at a height of 5 feet 6 inches (167 cm) and weighs 57 kg (125 lbs).

Aleisha Allen profession

Aleisha Allen is known as an actress who has appeared in different movies and TV shows. She started her career as an actress and model at a tender age as she worked as a print model before getting her first role in 1996 when she was cast in Nickelodeon’s show, Blue’s Clues: Arts & Crafts.

One of the most recognized roles she played was in the movie, Are We There Yet? Where she played alongside other stars like Nia Long, Ice Cube, and Philip Bolden.

Currently, she’s having 31 acting credits to her name which include Out of the Box, School of Rock, Blue’s Clues, The Best Man, Are We Done Yet?, Young Adult, Elmo’s Magic Cookbook, and others.

Where is Aleisha Allen now?

The actress was last featured in the film, You’re Nobody ’til Somebody Kills You in 2012 and now she works as a Speech Language Pathologist.
