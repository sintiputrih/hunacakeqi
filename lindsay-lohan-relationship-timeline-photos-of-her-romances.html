Lindsay Lohans Dating History: Aaron Carter, Samantha Ronson, More

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Lindsay Lohan’s romantic entanglements have inspired countless headlines for decades — beginning with her teenage flings.

During Lohan’s Disney days in the early 2000s, she was linked to the late Aaron Carter before dating That ‘70s Show alum Wilmer Valderrama. Lohan was later scrutinized for her relationship with English DJ Samantha Ronson and opened up to Us Weekly about the intense ridicule she received for dating a woman.

“I was bold enough to say, ‘Yeah, I like a girl. And?’ That put [Ronson] in a situation where she was being attacked every day,” the actress told Us in 2012. “That’s not fair. … And what am I left with? Heartbreak. I needed to love myself before I could be with anyone. And I was going through a lot. Two toxic people cannot be together. End of story.”

Lohan proceeded to keep a low-profile surrounding her dating life after calling off her engagement to Egor Tarabasov in 2016.
