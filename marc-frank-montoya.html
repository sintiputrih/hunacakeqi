Career

He is the first Mexican-American professional snowboarder to emerge from inner-city Denver, and he is the circuit’s only major Latino snowboarder. Since turning pro, he has appeared on nearly 300 pages of industry coverage.  Montoya was named “Rock Star of the Year” in the 2004 Transworld Magazine Rider’s Poll, and he was ranked third in Snowboard Magazine’slist of the top ten riders in 2005. He is best known for his urban style and park riding, especially rails. He is also involved in the development of various snowboarding brands and products, and he has a number of products with his sponsors.
