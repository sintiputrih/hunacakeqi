The Bachelor: Tierra's Eyebrow Gets Its Own Twitter Account

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Even though Tierra is gone from The Bachelor house following her umpteenth on-air meltdown, the controversial singleton will not soon be forgotten. In fact, the much talked-about contestant on this season of ABC's hit dating show is being memorialized online — in the form of a Twitter account dedicated exclusively to her oft-arched right eyebrow (her calmer left eyebrow does not currently have its own separate Twitter handle).

The hilarious account, aptly named @TierrasEyebrow, has been making pun-filled comments since its first tweet earlier on in the season. It presently has more than 19,000 followers.

"It didn't feel too good getting plucked out of the house like that," the anonymous user wrote on Tuesday, Feb. 12. Even former Bachelorette runner-up Arie Luyendyk got in on the fun, tweeting at Tierra's uncontrollable brow.

"Men love me" ..24 and a whole lot to learn," he wrote on Monday, Feb. 11. When another account, @TierrasSparkle, responded to his comment, he gamely shot back his own remark.

"So @TierrasSparkle is now tweeting me.. I'm concerned. I just now got over @TierrasEyebrow," he wrote jokingly.

On this week's episode, the 24-year-old Las Vegas native had the other remaining women in the house up in arms after she accused level-headed AshLee of trying to "sabotage" her relationship with bachelor Sean Lowe.

"I hope that when I'm 32 years old I don't have to sit around 20-year-olds gossiping," she sniped at AshLee. She also claimed that the other girls were constantly talking about the Houston native behind her back.

Later, however, when the pair took their argument to another room where Catherine and Lesley were lounging, Tierra denied having ever said such a thing, despite the fact that multiple cameras had recorded her accusations.

"You are rude," AshLee said sternly. "You do not say good morning, and your raised eyebrow…"

"Raised eyebrow? I cannot help my face," Tierra shot back. "I know in my own skin that I'm not rude. …My parents told me, 'Tierra, you have a sparkle. Do not let those girls take your sparkle away.' If I could walk around with a smile on my face 24/7 I would, but that would be tiring."

Even Lowe, who eventually decided that he couldn't deal with Tierra's emotional personality, couldn't resist taking a small jab at the ousted bachelorette.

When former Bachelorette winner Jef Holm jokingly tweeted at him: "Were you hanging out with Tierra & her uncontrollable eyebrow back there?" in reference to a photo Sean had shared, the Dallas hunk replied, "Yeah Tierra left but I wasn't ready to say goodbye to her eyebrow."
