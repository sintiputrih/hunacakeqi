Is ASAP Rocky Gay? Rappers Support for LGBTQ+ Community Sparks Rumors!

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

ASAP Rocky, the American rapper, is in the spotlight as fans want to know if he is gay. Many rumors and allegations imply that the rapper of “Thank the Lord” is gay.

Are all the rumors real, though? Or are they just untrue? Keep reading to learn the truth about ASAP Rocky’s sexual orientation!

Is ASAP Rocky Gay?

No, ASAP Rocky Is Not Gay. He is currently not dating any men and was never seen dating a guy in the past. Rocky is renowned for going back on his change of heart and criticizing other rappers for frequently utilizing homophobic and LGBT slurs in their lyrics. Hence, despite not being gay, Rocky unquestionably supports the LGBTQ+ community.

Is ASAP Rocky Single?

Rocky has a reputation for dating numerous women, but he is currently engaged to Grammy Award-winning artist Rihanna.

On May 13, Rocky and Rihanna gave birth to a boy, the couple’s first child. The Fenty Beauty tycoon and the hip-hop singer, who started dating in 2020, declared their desire to have children in January.

Even more thrillingly, she also made a fresh baby bump visible during the Super Bowl half-time show in February 2023! As this will be Rihanna and ASAP Rocky’s second child, fans are ecstatic!

ASAP Rocky’s Dating History

Throughout the past ten years, Rocky has been romantically linked to a number of other celebrities, including models, actors, and singers.

Iggy Azalea (2011-2012)

In 2011, Rocky and Australian musician Iggy Azalea began dating after becoming friends through a mutual acquaintance. Iggy went on to cross out the ‘A$AP’ section of her tattoo after the duo allegedly ended their romance later that year.

Chanel Iman (2013)

A$AP Early in 2013, Rocky began dating Victoria’s Secret model Chanel Iman. Iman enthusiastically accepted the rapper’s proposal in April 2014, according to reports that the couple was engaged.

Unfortunately, the couple called off their engagement and ended their relationship six months later due to their hectic travel schedules.

Tahiry Jose (2014- 2017)

During the time that ASAP Rocky was rumored to be dating Kendall Jenner, he was romantically associated with Tahiry Jose. Jose’s friend allegedly told Hollywood Life that Jose and Rocky had been having a “secretive” on-and-off romance for about three years.

She claimed that Jose was visibly upset the night of the 2017 Met Gala when Rocky was spotted cuddling up to Kendall. Yet neither party made the rumors official.

Kendall Jenner (2016-2017)

Before becoming romantically involved for the first time in 2016, Rocky and Kendall Jenner were longtime friends. Kendall faced the camera while Rocky gripped her behind with both hands as they appeared to be about to kiss.

It was said that they were completely smitten at Coachella in April 2020, and the rumors were all but confirmed at the Met Gala the following month. But neither individual ever confirmed the dating rumors in public.

Rihanna (2020-Present)

Shortly after sharing the red carpet at the Fashion Awards at London’s Royal Albert Hall, the lifelong friends were seen cuddling up at the 2020 Yams Day Benefit Concert in New York City. The rapper revealed their relationship for the first time in a GQ cover interview in May 2021, praising Rihanna as the one.
