What Is MMA Kamaru Usman Family Ethnicity? Explore His Net Worth And Kids Details

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, April 17, 2024

Kamaru Usman is a Nigerian-American competitive mixed martial artist, a previous freestyle fighter, and a folkstyle wrestler.

Kamaru Usman is the reigning UFC Welterweight Champion and he fights in the welterweight category of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC).

Usman was also the champion of The Ultimate Fighter 21 competition. He is presently ranked #1 in the UFC men’s pound-for-pound ratings as of September 27, 2021, and is recognized as one of the all-time best mixed martial artists.

His lone defeat came by surrender in his second professional battle, giving him a career record of 20-1. His primary weight class as a fighter was 84 kilos, and he was a part of the 2010 US University World Team.

Learn About Kamaru Usman Family And Ethnicity Details

Kamaru Usman was raised by his family in Auchi, Nigeria and they belong to the African-American ethnicity. 

Usman’s dad, Mohammad Usman brought his family to the United States when he was eight years old because he was suffering from the environment in Benin City. Mohammad was a Nigerian Army major, while his mom was a teacher.

Kashetu and Mohammed are his two brothers, the first of whom is a pharmacist and the latter a mixed martial artist as per the source.

Their father began working in the medical field. However, he was charged and imprisoned to ten years in jail for a variety of fraud crimes.

The welterweight victor has stated that these allegations are untrue and that his dad did not commit any offenses for which he should have been imprisoned.

Kamaru battled with the difficulties in his upbringing as a kid growing up in Benin City with his mom brothers.

MMA Fighter Kamaru Usman Net Worth Uncovered

Kamaru Usman has an estimated net worth of around $3 million. 

This is based on his present UFC contract, sponsorships, and multiple combat victories as a top-rated Welterweight competitor in the game.

From 2018, the 170-pound player’s actual UFC salary has increased significantly, with his bout against Jorge Masvidal on July 12th, 2020 paying him a whopping $1,423,333 dollars.

After his MMA journey, he is supposedly considering switching to boxing, with the possibility of high-paying matches against the other celebrity boxers.

Kamaru has two big sponsorship partnerships with Reebok and Trifecta, as well as a number of other commercial deals.

Meet Kamaru Usman’s Wife Eleslie Dietzsch: His Kids Details

Kamaru Usman is blissfully married to his wife Eleslie Dietzsch, and the couple has a gorgeous kid together.

Eleslie Dietzsch was born in Brazil on November 22, 1986, and she and her husband have a daughter named Samirah, who was born in 2014.

According to Wikipedia, Samirah is half-African, with her dad hailing from Nigeria, and half-Latin, and with her mom hailing from Brazil.

However, the legendary American fighter has been tight-lipped about his wife’s details.
