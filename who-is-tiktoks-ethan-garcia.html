Who is TikToks Ethan Garcia?

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Ethan Garcia is a popular content creator on TikTok with over 1.4 million followers on the platform, and an additional 300,000 on Instagram. His usernames are 3t1nn on the former, and ethangarciaaa on the latter.

Recommended Videos

According to a video published by Garcia on his birthday, 13 December 2022, the TikToker is currently 16 and a Sagittarius, whil it’s said he was born in California and grew up in Madera.

He grew his audience thanks to videos lip-syncing to famous TikTok sounds by artists like Playboi Carti and Drake, as well as acting out different scenarios, including comedy skits. Garcia frequently participates in popular trends, but most of his content hails from showcasing his looks. He has long curly black hair and wears braces.

Based on his videos, Garcia likes reggaeton music and hip-hop, as well as wearing dark clothes. The young TikToker also frequently appears alongside his cats on the page.

The comments under his videos all compliment his looks, and he seems to be very popular among his age group, which coincidentally constitutes a big part of TikTok’s demographic, and as a result, there are several fan pages for Garcia now both on TikTok and Instagram.

Garcia also has a second Instagram account and a second TikTok account, both with the username 3tonin. Additionally, he posts his fitness evolution on a gym-dedicated third TikTok account with the username wastedgeneticsp2. He’s friends with other popular TikTok users like William Hernandez III and luv4alexcito, but on his main account, Garcia has amassed almost 42 million likes.
