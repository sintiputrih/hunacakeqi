Mike Huckabee: A Brief Overview

Michael Dale Huckabee, born on August 24th, 1955, in Hope, Arkansas, USA, is an American Baptist preacher and politician. He served as the governor of Arkansas from 1996 to 2007 and has been actively involved in politics since 1993. Huckabee has recently been one of the Republican candidates for the 2016 presidential elections, and also ran in the 2008 elections, securing the third position. His accumulated net worth as of late 2015 was reported to be as high as $5 million.

About Mike Huckabee’s Wealth

After completing his BA from Ouachita Baptist University in 1975, Huckabee pursued theological studies at the South-Western Baptist Seminary. Eventually, he became involved in the Southern Baptist Convention as a pastor. In 1993, Huckabee was elected lieutenant governor of Arkansas. He later served as the governor of Arkansas, having been re-elected in 2000.

In 2008, Huckabee wrote a best-selling book called “Do The Right Thing: Inside the Movement That’s Bringing Common Sense Back to America.” Additionally, he hosted his own program, “Huckabee,” on Fox News from 2008 to 2015. Furthermore, Huckabee also had his own review section, “The Mike Huckabee Show,” on the ABC Radio Network from 2012 to 2013.

He announced his candidacy for the 2016 presidential elections on May 5, 2015. Huckabee’s political stance includes opposition to abortion, same-sex marriage, and embryonic stem cell research, and his support for the death penalty and the Iraq War. Personally, Huckabee married Janet in 1974, and they have three children. They reside in Santa Rosa Beach, Florida, USA.
