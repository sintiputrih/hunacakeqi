Isiah Pacheco parents: Julio Pacheco, Felicia Cannon

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, April 9, 2024

Isiah Pacheco is a famous American football running back for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League. He was picked as the second of three players in the seventh round of the NFL draft in April 2022.

He played football for the Cumberland County, New Jersey Vineland Blitz Pop Warner Football League when he was young. He attended Vineland High School, where he played high school football. 

Little is known about his personal life since he has decided to live a private life. He is the son of Julio Pacheco and his wife Felicia Cannon. In this article, we will dive into their personal lives to know more about them, especially their marriage and parental life. Continue reading to know Isiah’s parents.

Isiah Pacheco parents: Julio Pacheco, Felicia Cannon

Julio Pacheco and Felicia Cannon are celebrity parents who happen to be the parents of American football running back for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League, Isiah Pacheco. They are supportive parents who have been supportive of his career since day one of it.

They welcomed the footballer on  March 2, 1999. he is the last of three kids of the couple. The two elder children – a daughter and a son are no more since they passed away in a terrible accident.
