Make a Pokemon team for the above character!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, February 12, 2024
1. BMGF rules still apply. If you want to review it, click here.
2. Pokemon characters are banned, since they obviously have their own teams, apparently.
3. The characters' team should contain at least three Pokemon.
4. Try your best to make the Pokemon fit in the character's team. Also, if possible, add an explanation on why the team is good for his/her personality/ability/appearance/etc.
5. When posting a character, this should be the format:
  • Character (Name of game/anime/franchise)
6. Due to us having different levels of media knowledge, some of us cannot recognize the characters. If you cannot recognize the given character, research and make a Pokemon team out of the info you gather.
7. Have fun!
