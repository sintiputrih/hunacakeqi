The Concept Behind Better Bedder

Better Bedder is a unique headboard that acts as a giant headband for your mattress. It is designed to keep fitted sheets, flat sheets, and other bedding in place. The product was created out of the frustration of constantly fixing disheveled beds. It not only saves time but also ensures a neat and tidy bed with minimal effort.

Founders of Better Bedder

The minds behind Better Bedder are Judy Schott and Nita Gassen. These two entrepreneurs from Mandeville, Louisiana, turned their simple yet effective bedding solution into a business that caught the eye of the “Shark Tank” investors.
