Simon Cowell's son Eric looks identical to his older brother in new photo

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 25, 2024

The couple hosted their annual Easter bash

    Simon Cowell and Lauren Silverman hosted their annual Easter party this weekend! Sharing a number of snaps from the fun-filled bash, the couple’s close friend Terri Seymour took to Instagram to thank them for hosting. “Easter 2023. Thank you @simoncowell & #LaurenSilverman we love you guys!” she wrote in the caption.

    View post on Instagram 

    In the first photo, judge Simon posed with his fiancee Lauren, stepson Adam Silverman, and nine-year-old son, Eric. But the first thing we noticed was the uncanny resemblance between the half-brothers – Eric is practically Adam’s mini-me! 

    The siblings have always shared a close bond. They’ve been spotted holidaying together on several occasions, even wearing matching swim shorts on a family trip to Barbados, and they also enjoy family picnics and cycling jaunts together. While Lauren’s son Adam, whom she shares with ex Andrew Silverman, typically keeps out of the spotlight, it’s nice to see the family together again.

    Earlier this week, Simon’s good friend and Britain’s Got Talent co-star Amanda Holden revealed that she and Lauren had been touring universities together in the US. As Adam and Lexi (Amanda’s eldest daughter) have both turned 17, it looks like they’re planning to go to college. 

    Amanda and Lauren have grown extremely close over the years, so it's hardly surprising that they're touring colleges for their teens together. Posting a photo of Lexi and Adam on campus, Amanda wrote: "Their futures are bright…USA Uni…" 

    Amanda was also in attendance at Lauren and Simon’s Easter Soiree, which boasted an Easter egg hunt for the kids, and a lavish charcuterie board for grazing. Even the tables were decorated to perfection, with fresh flowers and adorable carrot ornaments making up the Easter-themed settings.
