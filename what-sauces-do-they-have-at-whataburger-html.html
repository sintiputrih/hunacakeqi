What exactly is sauce?

A sauce is a liquid, cream, or semi-solid meal that is placed on top of or used in the preparation of other dishes in the kitchen. Most sauces are not intended to be eaten on their own; rather, they are used to enhance the taste, wetness, and aesthetic appeal of a food. Sauce is a French term that comes from the Latin word salsa, which means salty food. Sauces need a liquid component to function properly.
