JLS Dancer Jack Pointer Died at The Age of 35

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Breaking news: JLS backup dancer Jack Pointer Mackenzie died on Monday. The announcement came to know from the social media post.

About Jack pointer Mackenzie

Jack was a professional choreographer, editor, and an artist. He has worked within the film throughout his life. His dedication always uplifts the brand. He always captured the true essence of brands in his every project. Recently, he worked as a leading cameraman with Adidas and Paco Rabanne on their new campaign
Jack pointer connection with JLS

Jack Pointer Mackenzie was connected to the JLS, an English boy band, and was toured with JLS throughout their career, including from X Factor in 2009 to the last tour in 2021.
