Who Rocked Latex Dress Better Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 18, 2024


Kim wore her first Atsuko Kudo Paris Cup pink latex dress first at her Fleur Fatale fragrance launch at Spice Market on Nov. 18, 2014, in Melbourne, Australia. That was just the beginning of a latex obsession. She was also wearing a pink latex dress with a plunging neckline, by the same designer, at the Hype Energy Drinks launch party on June 2 in Nashville.

As if those two dresses weren’t enough, she attended the Mert & Marcus House of Love party at Annabel’s in London on Feb. 26 in the Atsuko Kudo Sleeveless Joy Pencil Dress, which she paired with a fur coat and nude Manolo Blahnik strappy sandals — so this definitely wasn’t Kim’s first rodeo.

Tracee however, has just experimented with the latex trend for the first time at the 2015 BET Awards, where she was the hostess. She paired the turtle-neck dress with nude pointy-toed pumps and a giant gold bangle.

We think both of these curvaceous ladies look fabulous in the dress, but to be honest it resembles more of a costume than anything else. Latex just isn’t to be a fabric — do you agree?

We can’t decide who rocked the dress better! Who do you think wore latex better, Hollywoodlifers? VOTE
