Jaxon Bieber Bio: Age, Siblings, Parents &amp; Pictures

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Jaxon Bieber is a Canadian Instagram star and social media sensation born on November 20, 2009.

He is best known as the young half brother of the Canadian pop singer, Justin Bieber.

Jaxon as fondly called has earned over one million followers on Instagram.

His parents are Jeremy Bieber who is also justin beiber’s father and mother, Erin Wagner(Jeremy’s second wife).

He also has an elder sister named Jazmyn Bieber. He is also close to his step sister, Allie Rebelo.

Jaxon is the step son of Pattie Mallette who has a net worth of $20 million dollars.

His fame came as a result of being one of the siblings of Justin Bieber.

Often called different names like ‘Little Justin’, ‘Little Bieber’ and so on, he immediately became a public figure following the rise to stardom of Justin Bieber.

Jaxon Bieber is a fun loving person. He loves to listen to songs and also Biking.
