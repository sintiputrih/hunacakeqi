Alexa Chung Selects Her Father as a Date for Various Occasions

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, March 18, 2024

Alexa Chung is a fashion industry trendsetter known for her sense of style. She is well-rounded as a model, author, TV presenter, and designer of her own clothing line. Aside from staying close to her passion in the glamor industry, the fashionista is also very close to her family, as evidenced by their attendance at her shows.

Not only that, but her father, who is her biggest fan, has even accompanied her as her date to various events. A quick look through his Instagram shows that almost every other post is about his daughter Alexa.

Alexa brought her father to meet the Duchess of Cambridge

Phil Chung, Alexa Chung’s father, is very active on social media and frequently posts about how proud he is of his daughter’s accomplishments. He even expressed his enthusiasm for her Netflix show Next in Fashion.

Similarly, the fashionista has a special bond with her grandfather, as evidenced by her use of him as a date at various events. In 2019, Alexa accompanied her father to The Royal Academy Of Arts Summer Exhibition preview party and The Portrait Gala.

She even took him to the fourth annual National Portrait Gallery Gala, where they met Kate Middleton, the Duchess of Cambridge. In her Instagram stories, she can be seen preparing her father for his meeting with the Duchess. He did get to speak with her, and they talked about children’s painting, and he advised her, being a painter himself, to collect all of her children’s paintings.

Phil, Alexa’s father, is three-quarters Chinese

Alexa is of mixed ethnicity, having been born and raised in England by an English mother and raised in China by a Chinese-English father. Phil Chung, her father, is three-quarters Chinese, and her mother, Gill Chung, is entirely English.

Phil is a retired graphic designer who paints house portraits, while Gill is a stay-at-home mom. Alexa’s parents’ relationship appears to be intact, as they have been seen attending her shows together.

Alexa regards herself as a brat because she is the youngest of the siblings

The 37-year-old model is the youngest of four siblings, including two brothers, Jamie and Dom, and a sister, Natalie. She described herself as a brat in an interview with the Independent. “As the youngest of four, I think it’s fair to say I’m the brat,” she explained. I’m the one who asked for a pony, was picky about meat, and was always looking for attention.”

Alexa’s siblings are all married with children of their own. Her brother Jamie is a photographer, and Dom was a DJ at the ALEXACHUNG Fantastic collection party in 2018. Her family is close to her, as evidenced by their attendance at her shows, and they even went on vacation to Italy together in 2018.

Alexa Began Her Career As A Model Before Becoming A Fashion Designer

Alexa was scouted as a model at the age of 16 and worked in the modeling industry for four years before becoming a fashion designer. She has graced the covers of Vogue and served as the face of brands such as Superga and Longchamp.

After that, she decided to branch out and began working as a TV host. She went on to work for Channel 4 and hosted the shows 8 Out of 10 Cats and Big Brother’s Big Mouth. Alexa also hosted her own MTV show, It’s On with Alexa Chung.

She published her first book, ‘It,’ in 2013, in which she shares her personal inspirations, musings, and electric sense of style. It consists of writing, doodles, and photographs. She has also been a contributing editor for British Vogue and written for numerous magazines. The fashionista is now preoccupied with her high-end label, Alexa Chung, which debuted in May 2017.

Alexa Chung Net Worth

Alexa Chung’s net worth is about $12 million as of December 2023.
