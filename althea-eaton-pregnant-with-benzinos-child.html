Althea Eaton: Pregnant with Benzino's Child!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, February 2, 2024

If you watch Love and Hip Hop Atlanta online, you know Benzino has been through a lot lately. Well, he just got an amazing piece of good news:

Althea Eaton is pregnant with the couple’s first child!

She’s five months along, and expecting a baby boy in November. This is the real deal, according to TMZ, unlike the last time this rumor came up.

Pregnancy chatter began in March, when Althea blasted the news on social media, only to see Benzino delete the pregnancy announcement.

Neither of them have spoken about it since.

Now it’s official, though, and the fact that Althea is pregnant is certainly a huge step for a duo that seemed to be hanging by a thread not long ago.

Their tumultuous relationship was well documented even before the infamous Love and Hip Hop Atlanta reunion fight that ended Season 3.

In the wake of the brawl (below) and death threats against co-stars, VH1 cut the couple loose from the cast before the current Season 4.

More time to devote to their little one, at least? And if you’re gonna get kicked off a reality show, you might as well go out like this … right?
