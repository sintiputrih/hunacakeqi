Angelina Jolie's Brother Broke Silence About Their Close Relationship

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, January 25, 2024

Angelina Jolie has always shared a close bond with her older brother, James Haven, but it’s rare to hear him make any commentary about his very famous sister. The 52-year-old film festival director broke his silence about their relationship and gave fans quite a bit of insight about what is happening behind the scenes amid her ongoing drama with ex-husband Brad Pitt.

Host Jessica Entner of the 90Who10 podcast didn’t shy away from asking Haven about his involvement with Jolie and her six kids, Maddox, 22, Pax, 20, Zahara, 18, Shiloh, 17, and twins Knox and Vivienne, 15, as they navigated the fallout from Pitt’s alleged domestic violence incident in 2016 which “unfolded in the public eye.” “That’s where it all started, it started with the protection of her and then of her children, my nieces and nephews,” Haven shared. “There are those are massively formative years; they’re becoming young adults in their early 20s.” Related story Insiders Reveal the Reason Angelina Jolie & Brad Pitt’s Kids Reportedly ‘Get Into Arguments’ Over This Subject Amid Their Parents’ Divorce

Haven is not only present for his sister and the kids, but he also stepped up as a father figure. “That’s so crazy thinking that, but yeah, I think it’s just very natural [to feel protective] and I just want to be there. Very much like my mom [Marcheline Bertrand]. Anytime I’m blessed to be in their presence, I want to be in their presence,” he continued. “I set my life up so I can be present with whatever the situation. I want to be there for them or for her — whatever she’s going through.”

His presence was probably a welcome relief to Jolie who has hinted at the reported abuse and trauma she and the kids suffered through during Pitt’s battle with alcohol. She’s primarily let the court documents tell their side of the horrific story which involved the 2016 private plane incident where the Thelma and Louise star allegedly “choked one of the children and struck another in the face,” per court documents obtained by The New York Times. The altercation continued when Pitt “grabbed Jolie by the head and shook her” and “at one point, he poured beer on Jolie; at another, he poured beer and red wine on the children,” per the legal filing. It’s fortunate that Jolie and her children had Haven to lean on.

Haven usually stays out of the spotlight, but he is probably best remembered for Jolie’s Girl Interrupted award show season in 2000 where they seemed a little too close for comfort. They were often snapped sharing kisses on the mouth that some critics felt went beyond what Jolie described as “brotherly love.” It turned out to be much ado about nothing as the siblings have proven that their close bond is about family ties and sticking together no matter how tough things get.
