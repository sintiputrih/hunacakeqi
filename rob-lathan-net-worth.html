Rob Lathan Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Rob Lathan is an American comedian based in New York City. He has been a sketch character actor on shows such as Late Night with Jimmy Fallon, The Colbert Report, Late Night with Conan O'Brien, Human Giant and Best Week Ever.Lathan performs at The Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre.Lathan is a senior member of the comedy group Improv Everywhere, having performed in several of the groups public comedic performances. He has starred in multiple missions, including getting lost all over Yankee Stadium, giving 2,000 high fives to New York commuters by standing on a stairway next to a subway escalator and as a Ghostbuster going through the NYC Library.Lathan hosts a show called Get Psyched! in which he plays a life coach named Dr. Lanny Latham.Lathan attended The Westminster Schools in Atlanta, Georgia and the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
