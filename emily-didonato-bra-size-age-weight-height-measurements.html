Emily DiDonato Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 28, 2024

Biography:

Emily DiDonato was born on February 24, 1991 in New York, USA. After she was encouraged to pursue modeling by a family friend, she signed with Request Model Management in 2008.

The following year, she became the face of Guess? and a model in Ralph Lauren’s “Rugby” spring 2009 ad campaign. In addition, she was signed as the face of Maybelline New York.

She then went on to make her television commercial debut for Maybelline New York’s “Color Sensational Lip Color” line, alongside prominent models Christy Turlington, Jessica White, and Julia Stegner.
