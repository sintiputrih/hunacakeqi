Career

Madilyn Bailey started her career as a YouTuber by posting the covers of popular songs on her YouTube channel and amassed over 8.4 million subscribers and 100 million views on her channel in no time.

After the completion of her graduation, she joined Keep Your Soul Records to assist with the production of her material, after which she moved to Los Angeles in the year 2013.

In the latter half of the year 2013, she toured the United States and Canada with a popular American cover band named Boyce Avenue. In the year 2015, she released her rendition of the song titled Titanium and gained airplay on Virgin Radio in France. After that, she signed a contract with Warner Music Group’s label PlayOn and released an album of covers titled Muse Box with singles like Rude and Believe.

In the year 2016, she released an extended play titled Wiser with five tracks. In the same year, her cover of the song Titanium was featured on the Australian television series titled Wanted. 

In the year 2018, she has been featured on the popular American talk show titled Today where she performed the live version of the single titled Tetris.

In the same year, her single titled Drunk on a Feeling has been featured on the popular American action-drama television series titled Station 19. Along with these, she has released various original singles like Red Ribbon, All My Oxygen, Tasted Like Karma, How Good We Had It, and others.
