Hire Taylor Tomlinson for a Corporate Event or Performance Booking.

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 8, 2024
Sam Tomlinson is a gifted comedian who has gained recognition for her entertaining performances and personable demeanor. Her father put her in a stand-up class when she was 16 years old, sparking her interest in comedy and her desire to make others laugh. Tomlinson began her career in the comedy industry with little notoriety, honing her craft in coffee shops, schools, and basements of churches.

She displayed her skills as a top 10 finalist on the ninth season of NBC's Last Comic Standing in 2015. She had the honor of being named one of Variety's "Top 10 Comics to Watch" at the 2019 Just for Laughs Festival thanks to her comic prowess. Tomlinson has demonstrated her ability to enthrall audiences with her wit and humor on a number of Comedy Central programs as well as well-known talk shows including The Tonight Show and Conan.

Tomlinson has experimented with a variety of platforms to showcase her humorous brilliance throughout her career. In 2017, she created a sitcom for ABC, showcasing her range as a comic author. She further cemented her status as a rising star in the comedy industry with her 15-minute act on the Netflix stand-up series The Comedy Lineup in 2018.

Quarter-Life Crisis, Tomlinson's debut Netflix stand-up special, debuted in March 2020 to positive reviews. Later that year, she and fellow comedian Whitney Cummings set out on the Codependent Tour, wowing audiences with their hilarious performances. Tomlinson had a significant impact on podcasts as well since, together with fellow comedians Kelsey Cook and Delanie Fischer, she co-hosted the well-known podcast Self-Helpless.

Tomlinson started her own podcast, Sad in the City, in 2021, where she explores many subjects with her distinct humor and perceptive viewpoints. When she was given a spot on the Forbes 30 Under 30 list in December 2021, her talent and influence were celebrated..
