Has Cardi B ever done a sneaker collab?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 31, 2024

Cardi B, the renowned hip-hop superstar, has made a significant mark in the sneaker world through her successful collaborations with Reebok. Her influence in the music industry has transcended into the realm of fashion and footwear, solidifying her status as a multifaceted icon.

Cardi B's Collaboration with Reebok

Cardi B's partnership with Reebok has been a resounding success, with the introduction of a diverse range of footwear and apparel collections. The collaboration commenced in 2018 and has since evolved into multiple releases, each reflecting Cardi B's unique style and New York roots. The capsule collection, titled "Let Me Be…In My World," pays homage to Cardi B's hometown, New York City, and features a blend of trendy and versatile pieces, including tight fits and oversized details. The collection is set to launch on August 27, 2024, further expanding the reach of Cardi B's fashion influence.

Reebok x Cardi B Collaborations Overview

  • First Apparel Collection: In April, Cardi B released her first apparel collection with Reebok, known as the Summertime Fine Collection. This collection drew inspiration from '90s fashion and Cardi B's cherished summer memories at Coney Island, N.Y. The collection included athleisure pieces designed in pastel purple and red color palette, showcasing Cardi B's personal style and fashion preferences.

  • Mommy & Me Collection: Following the initial success, Cardi B launched a "Mommy & Me" collection with Reebok in May, featuring monochrome versions of the Club C Cardi sneaker in adult and kids' sizes. This collection was inspired by Cardi B's daughter, Kulture, reflecting the personal touch that Cardi B incorporates into her collaborations.

  • Upcoming Collection: The upcoming "Let Me Be…In My World" collection is poised to offer sizes ranging from 2XS to 4X, catering to a diverse audience and demonstrating Cardi B's commitment to inclusivity in fashion.

Cardi B's Influence on Reebok Campaigns

Cardi B's partnership with Reebok extends beyond product collaborations, as she has been actively involved in promoting the brand's campaigns. Her involvement in the promotion of the Aztrek runner from the '90s and other Reebok products has further solidified her role as an influential figure in the sneaker industry. The brand's official press release acknowledges Cardi B's multifaceted persona, highlighting her as a mother, style icon, and pop culture magnet. This recognition underscores the impactful role that Cardi B plays in shaping fashion and sneaker trends.

Cardi B's foray into sneaker collaborations with Reebok has been characterized by innovative designs, personal touches, and an unwavering commitment to inclusivity and versatility. Her influence has transcended the boundaries of the music industry, further establishing her as a prominent figure in the fashion and sneaker world. As Cardi B continues to expand her collaborations with Reebok, her impact on the sneaker industry is set to resonate with a diverse audience, reflecting the dynamic and multifaceted nature of her brand.
