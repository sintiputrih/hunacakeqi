Who Is Maurizio Gucci Girlfriend Paola Franchi And Where Is She Now?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

Maurizio Gucci had just started his relationship with his girlfriend, Paola Franchi, when he was killed by a plan of his ex-wife. Here is what you need to know about him. 

Paola Franchi is an Italian professional interior designer, former model, artist, and author who is recognized for her relationship with later businessman and designer Maurizio Gucci. 

Paola and Maurizio had just been together for a brief period of time when he was brutally killed, and they never got to have a long-running relationship. 

Well, Paola was not a very renowned personality by herself or because of her job, but she got her initial fame as the partner of the world-renowned person, Maurizio Gucci. 

Despite over 2 and a half-decade of his murder and the incident happening, people are still eager to find out more about his then-girlfriend. 

Indeed, concerns regarding her age, whereabouts, Wikipedia, and other relevant matters are at their peak on the web right now and are constantly rising all over.

Who Is Maurizio Gucci Girlfriend Paola Franchi Age And Wikipedia

The age of  Maurizio Gucci’s girlfriend Paola Franchi is 68 years old now. 

She was born on November 17, 1953, in Milan, Italy, and she is still alive and healthy. 

Talking about Wikipedia, she is mentioned on the platform because her partner was a very popular personality, and she was a great woman herself too. 

As mentioned, she is initially an interior designer and a model, but after her relationship with the descendent of the Gucci founder’s family and the one-time head of the Gucci fashion house, Maurizio Gucci made her even more famous. 

When she started her relationship with Maurizio, his former wife hired a hitman and killed him, because of which they could not have a long relationship. 

A few years after this happened, her son passed away by suicide and she was even sadder. But this led her to start a charity organization nd also write a book about her life.

Where Is Paola Franchi Now In 2021? 

Paola Franchi is alive and she is probably living somewhere in Italy right now. 

The last time people saw her officially in the public was over 10 years ago when the documentary about her aired. 

She has not been seen officially then, but as per reports, she is alive and good. As mentioned, she started a charity named L’Amico Charly after her son died. 

This charity is still working, which means that she still might be a part of the charity that she made in remembrance of her son. 

Also, her book named L’Amore Spezzato, which translates to Broken Love, is also a popular book until now. Her legacy has stayed, even if she is not personally seen anywhere on the media platforms.
