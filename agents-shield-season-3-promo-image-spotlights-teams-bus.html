Agents Of S.H.I.E.L.D. Season 3 Promo Image Spotlights The Teams New Bus

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, January 1, 0001

Recommended Videos

During the first two seasons of Marvel’s Agents Of S.H.I.E.L.D., Coulson and his crew travelled in The S.H.I.E.L.D. Globemaster: a modified Boeing C-17 Globemaster III military transport aircraft which was more commonly referred to as, “The Bus.” It was quite an impressive aircraft, but the team was forced to sacrifice it during  S.H.I.E.L.D.’s attack on the Arctic HYDRA Research Facility.

Well, Marvel has now released an image of their new bus, and it seems our heroes have been given an upgrade. From the looks of it, the Zephyr One is a Quinjet – the incredibly powerful preferred mode of transport of The Avengers.

[zergpaid]

In addition, we also have the synopsis for the third episode back, “A Wanted (Inhu)man,” and it seems Hunter is having a hard time forgetting what Ward put Mockingbird through during those intense torture scenes back in season 2:

A Wanted (Inhu)man” – Daisy, Mack and Coulson race to protect Lincoln as Rosalind’s team hunts down the Inhuman in their quest for powered people. Meanwhile, Hunter proves that there is no line that he will not cross to exact his revenge against Ward and Hydra.

Agents Of S.H.I.E.L.D. returns for its third season tomorrow night with “Laws Of Nature.”
