Cole Bennett | Career

Along with live event recaps, ciphers, documentaries, and interviews, Cole started directing music videos for Chicago rappers like Vic Mensa and Taylor Bennett.

He posted the videos on the Lyrical Lemonade channel.

The channel later branched into other hip-hop subgenres outside the Chicago area, like the newly growing Soundcloud rap sub-genre.

He garnered early fame in 2016 and early 2017 by working with musicians like Lil Pump, Famous Dex, Smokepurpp, and Ski Mask the Slump God.

“Famous Dex: Hit Em Wit It,” posted in March 2016, was his first video to become famous. More than 20 million people watched the music video.

People adored his videos’ bizarre animations. His unique designs put him on the map.

Later, he published “Lone Springs,” his debut short film, on April 7, 2017.

Bennett directed the music video in August 2017 for Lil Xan’s song “Betrayed,” which the RIAA certified Platinum in 2018.
