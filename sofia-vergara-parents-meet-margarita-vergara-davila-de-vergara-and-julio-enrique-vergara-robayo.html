Sofia Vergara Parents: Meet Margarita Vergara Dvila de Vergara And Julio Enrique Vergara Robayo

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, March 11, 2024

Sofía Margarita Vergara Vergara is a Colombian and American actress and model. From 2013 to 2020, she was the highest paid actress in American television.

Vergara rose to fame while co-hosting two television shows for Spanish-language television network Univision in the late 1990s.

Her first notable acting job in English was in the film Chasing Papi (2003), after which she appeared in;

  • Four Brothers (2005) and
  • Tyler Perry’s comedies Meet the Browns (2008) and Madea Goes to Jail (2009), receiving an ALMA Award nomination for the latter.

    • Vergara was born on July 10, 1972 to parents Margarita Vergara Dávila de Vergara and Julio Enrique Vergara Robayoa.

    Meet Margarita Vergara Dávila de Vergara And Julio Enrique Vergara Robayo

    Vergara’s parents are from a Roman Catholic family in Barranquilla and she was raised such as.

    Margarita Vergara Dávila de Vergara

    Vergara’s mum Margarita Vergara Dávila de Vergara was a homemaker.

    In 1992, Margarita ‘s marriage with Julio was dissolved and in the late 1990s, Margarita moved from Columbia to the US to be closer to her daughter, who considers her mom to be her role model.

    In one of Sofia’s interviews, she revealed that her mother forced her to be a model.

    Julio Enrique Vergara Robayo

    Vergara’s father Julio Enrique Vergara Robayo was a cattle rancher for the meat industry.

    He was born on 1948 and died in December aged 61.

    He wanted Sofia to be a dentist and that’s why, she studied dentistry for three years before moving to America.

    Julio had a stroke in 2007, which resulted in coma.

    He passed away on December 18, 2009.
