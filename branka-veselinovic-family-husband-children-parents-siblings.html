Branka Veselinovi family, husband, children, parents, siblings

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, March 15, 2024

Branka Veselinović was a Serbian actress loved by many following her great acting skills and personality. She was born to her parents on September 16, 1918, in Bečej, Serbia but sadly passed away on Wednesday, February 8, 2023, at the age of 104.

In this article, we will dive into her personal life to know more about her, especially her family to know her husband, children, parents and siblings.

Table of Content

Branka Veselinović husband

She was a widow as of her time of death. She was married to actor Mlađa Veselinović. The two were married from 1948 to 2012 after the actor passed away.

Branka Veselinović children

She was not a mother as of her time of death. She had no biological child and did not adopt any child as well.

Branka Veselinović parents

She is the daughter of Aleksandar Ćosić and Jovanka Ćosić.  Her father Aleksandar was a librarian while her mother, Jovanka was a teacher.

Branka Veselinović siblings

She is one of seven children of her parents and happens to be the sixth child, hence, he has a younger sibling and five older siblings. The identities of her siblings are yet to be revealed.
