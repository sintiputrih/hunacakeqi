Larry Larson is a renowned American car racer famous lately for his appearance on Discovery’s Street Outlaws: NO PREP KINGS.

Larry was among the 25 street racers revving engines on the all-new season of the competition series that premiered on 11 October 2021.

With that being said read through this ‘Larry Larson BIO’ to uncover the rest.

Larry Larson On Street Outlaws

With the Street Outlaws‘ very elements (the louder call-outs, riskier racing, and full-throttle thrills) put aside some of the shows’ fans were rather occupied by the whole Larry Larson and Jeff Lutz drama. Even these stars of the show looked like they have extended the rivalry beyond the starting line of a race and into their day-to-day lives.

Larry of Team X allegedly said that he simply just does not like Jeff, as per the war of words over Reddit. One contributor to this conflict stated that Larry actually won Drag Week five years in a row some time ago, and Jeff was only able to secure his first victory after Larry stopped competing and reasoned this very fact to have caused their feud.

Nonetheless, this rivalry had not ever boiled over into any one-on-one verbal or physical confrontation and was expected to only ever heat up on the pavement.

In the meantime, there are always good things to look at on Stree Outlaws, especially now with more teams in the mix, new cars making their debut, more racing locations than ever, and new faces stepping into this side of the world.

Larry Larson Job Beyond Street Outlaws

Larry Larson is the proud owner/president of this local business that he named Larson Race Cars during its establishment in November 1996. Yes. Larry has been in the business for that long!

We all should know that Larry is a premier race and street performance car builder with over 40 years of experience in racing. His Larson Race Cars already seemed to have pleased some 81.475K people on Facebook @LarsonRaceCars.

As of 2018, he drove a Cadillac ATS-V and it was built all by himself. The Cadillac was built at Larson Race Cars, which has been seen regularly on Street Outlaws: No Prep Kings. And since this very discovery, Larry also has been competing in the NPK competition.
