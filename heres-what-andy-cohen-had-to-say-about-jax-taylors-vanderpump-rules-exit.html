Here's What Andy Cohen Had To Say About Jax Taylor's Vanderpump Rules Exit

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Jax Taylor and Brittany Cartwright's relationship may have been one of the major storylines on Vanderpump Rules, but Andy Cohen reminded everyone that it was Jax who was an OG Vanderpump Rules star, noting that he created "so much story, drama, conversation in his eight years on that show." "That's quite a run that he had," Cohen said on his radio show. "He will go down as one of the biggest, you know, reality stars in their moment, don't you think?" Additionally, Cohen commended Jax for being "a good sport" when he appeared on Watch What Happens Live. "He was always in the middle of some s**tstorm on the show and we just would roast him mercilessly, and he always took it like a man."

Echoing his remarks in Jax's Instagram comments, Cohen wrote (via Reality Blurb), "Incredible run, dude. Amazing show and you were always the best, greatest sport. I have loved having you on wwhl and being on this ride with you." Seemingly touched by his former boss's words, Jax shared a screenshot of the comment to his Instagram Story, writing, "Thanks @bravoandy I'll see you again soon .."

Despite Jax and Brittany's departure, Cohen said he's "excited for there to be a shift" with his new "solid cast." Back in June 2020, Stassi Schroeder, Kristen Doute, Max Boyens and Brett Caprioni got the boot from Bravo following various accusations of racism. As of this writing, Season 9's cast has yet to be announced.
