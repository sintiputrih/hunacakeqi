Is there a barber shop in Wrath of the Lich King?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Yes, there are barbershops located in Stormwind, Orgrimmar, and Dalaran in Wrath of the Lich King.

Can you change your hair in Wrath of the Lich King?

Yes, in Modern WoW and Wrath of the Lich King Classic, you can change your character’s appearance at the barber shop. This includes your character’s gender, hair, facial features, and skin color. However, you cannot change your character’s name or race.

Is there a barber in Valdrakken WoW?

No, there is no barber shop in Valdrakken in Wrath of the Lich King.

Where to get a haircut in WoW classic?

In WoW classic, you can get a haircut at various barbershop locations:

  • Northshear Abbey, Trade District, Stormwind City
  • The Commons, Ironforge
  • Nip and Tusk, Orgrimmar
  • Trade Quarter, Undercity
  • Dalaran
  • Area 52, Netherstorm
  • Cosmowrench, Netherstorm

Can you change haircut in classic WoW?

In classic WoW, the only way to change your hairstyle is by deleting your current character and creating a new one with the desired hair style. This is because choices made in character creation in Classic WoW actually matter and cannot be changed after creation.
