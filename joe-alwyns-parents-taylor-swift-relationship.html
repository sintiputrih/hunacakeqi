Why Joe Alwyn's Mysterious Parents Were Almost Never Spotted Anywhere Near Taylor Swift

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn shared an ultra-private love life that extended to their relationship with their parents.
  • Alwyn's parents, despite approving of Swift, remained private and were rarely seen with the music icon.
  • Swift and Alwyn dated for six years but ultimately split due to differences in personalities and handling fame.

It goes without saying that being associated with Taylor Swift will naturally cast one in the spotlight. This is evident in her highly publicized relationship with NFL star Travis Kelce and her past relationships. When Swift dated Joe Alwyn, their relationship was pretty much private, with only a few moments of their public appearances and insider information getting out. Part of the details of their relationship was how the "Folklore" crooner and the Young Margaret actor's families approved of their love life.

Despite the news that their families adored their relationship, fans could not help but notice that the music icon was barely photographed with Joe Alwyn's parents. Notably, during the former couple's relationship, they adopted an ultra-private love life, which might have extended to their relationship with their parents. The songstress, whose 11th studio album "The Tortured Poets Department" is believed to be inspired by Alwyn, dated him for six years. In this article, we will examine why Alwyn's mom and dad were barely spotted with Swift throughout their relationship. We will also examine the superstar's rapport with the senior Alwyns.

Inside Taylor Swift's Private Relationship With Joe Alwyn's Parents

When former lovers Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn debuted their relationship in 2016, they received their parents' blessings. One month after the news of their romance made it to the public, Alwyn traveled with the Reputation crooner to Nashville, where he met her parents. At the time, an insider revealed to Life & Style that Swift's parents approved of the actor, and he took the time to meet every member of her family. The source added that the singer's parents were glad she "finally met a gentleman."

Similarly, Taylor Swift visited Joe Alwyn's parents in the United Kingdom, where she was well-received. However, not much about her relationship with his parents was reflected in how much time they spent together in public.

Swift was rarely photographed with Alwyn's parents, and they seldom attended her world-famous concerts and tours. Despite the couple's private love life, Alwyn openly supported his lover by attending her events and even penning Grammy-winning songs with her. Still, his parents opted for a more private rapport.

Back in 2018, during the opening night of Taylor Swift's Reputation tour, her beloved boyfriend could be seen in the audience, albeit concealing his face with a baseball cap. Joe Alwyn's siblings and Swift's family members were also present. While his parents may have been at the show, they were not seen with the other family members.

Swift was just as supportive of Joe Alwyn's movies and career as a whole, making sure to attend award shows with him. She even once ditched the Grammys to attend the BAFTA Awards with him. However, Alwyn's parents were notably absent. Nonetheless, there was a rare moment in London when Swift was spotted on the streets of London with Alwyn and his mom.

Did Taylor Swift Get Along With Joe Alwyn's Parents?

The "Midnights" singer's relationship with Joe Alwyn's parents was mostly out of the spotlight, but did she get along with them? According to insiders, Alwyn's family adored the singer, and she enjoyed spending time with them. 2021 Taylor Swift celebrated New Year's Eve in England with his family. E! News reported that she split her time between Nashville and England to be able to spend time with Alwyn's family and hers.

During the early months of the relationship, the 1989 crooner was said to have desperately wanted her parents to get along with Joe Alwyn's parents. Taylor Swift flew Joe Alwyn's parents to Nashville to meet her folks. According to a Life & Style magazine source, Swift offered to fly Elizabeth and Richard Alwyn via her private jet, so they could spend time with their mom and dad. The insider relayed:

"Taylor is extremely close to her family, and she loves Joe's parents. She really wants them all to get to know each other."
